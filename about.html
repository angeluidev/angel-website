I’m Angelica, but you can call me Angel! I’m an aspiring UX / UI Designer currently living in Singapore.

My design philosophy is informed by my values as a person. Many of those are centered around kindness and empathy. I also firmly believe in the power of research and data in order to better understand humans and design for scale and inclusion.

I also enjoy the challenge of front-end programming. I’m gritty, hardworking and continuously learning.

HER-STORY

Let's get personal

I started my career as a Journalist in the Philippines, covering natural disasters to corruption scandals.


A year into the job, I had a life-changing experience which led me to realize that it’s unfair to just take stories from people without helping to improve their lives.

I wanted to combine my passion for creativity, logical thinking and altruistic nature.


In 2018, I came across the concept of design thinking when I was listening to a Google TechTalks Podcast and that opened the doors of discovery to the world of UX / UI design and development.
